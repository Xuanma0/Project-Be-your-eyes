# Spatial Audio Demo
In the demo,I add a simple audio source(cube in the scene).The cube will display a cliff of music,and the camera will receive the audio.I've added Meta XR Audio SDK(in the unity package),and enabled it.

In the audio source Inspector ,you can see several parameters.
- Firstly,enable spatial audio,then drag the slider of "blend" to the right,labelled "3D"
Unfold the 3D Sound Settings,you will see:
- Rolloff Mode:I have set it to linear mode.
- Min Distance
- Max Distance

I  can't simply measure the delay because there exists other Parts.
